About Calamba

Calamba is a first-class component city in the province of Laguna, Philippines. It is the regional center of the CALABARZON region. Celebrated as the birthplace of the Philippine national hero, Dr. Jose Rizal, Calamba seamlessly blends rich historical heritage with natural wonders. It is renowned nationwide for its numerous hot spring resorts fed by the geothermal waters of Mount Makiling, making it the perfect destination for both history enthusiasts and relaxation seekers.
