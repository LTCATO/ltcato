Wonderland Hot Spring

Calamba City, Laguna

About this Destination

Nestled at the foothills of Mount Makiling, Wonderland Hot Spring offers a therapeutic escape with its naturally heated thermal pools. Known for its rich mineral waters, this resort has been a favorite destination for families and travelers seeking relaxation and wellness in the heart of Calamba City.

The resort features multiple pools varying in temperature, private cottages, and lush garden landscapes. It's the perfect place to unwind after a long week, offering both day tours and overnight accommodations.
