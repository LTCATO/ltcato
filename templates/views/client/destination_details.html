Hidden Gems of Iceland: Beyond the Golden Circle

Reykjavik, Iceland

Description

Discover the hidden gems of Iceland that lie beyond the popular Golden Circle route. This extraordinary journey takes you through some of Iceland's most breathtaking landscapes, from dramatic waterfalls and geothermal hot springs to remote highlands and pristine glaciers. Experience the raw beauty of nature in its purest form as you explore volcanic craters, black sand beaches, and the mystical Northern Lights dancing across the Arctic sky.

Our expert guides will lead you through ancient lava fields, share fascinating stories about Icelandic folklore, and introduce you to local traditions that have been preserved for centuries. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, this off-the-beaten-path experience promises memories that will last a lifetime.

What's Included:

  • Professional local guide
  • Small group experience (max 12 people)
  • Transportation in modified 4x4 vehicle
  • Traditional Icelandic lunch
  • Photography assistance
  • Thermal gear and safety equipment
