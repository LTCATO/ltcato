Why You'll Love It

Escape to Nature's Perfect Oasis

Imagine sinking into crystal-clear, mineral-rich waters that naturally warm your body while tropical breezes kiss your skin. This isn't just a resort—it's your personal sanctuary where stress melts away and memories are born.

What It Is

Wonderland Hot Spring is a premier natural thermal resort nestled at the foothills of Mount Makiling in Calamba City. Featuring multiple temperature-controlled pools fed by natural hot springs, this 15+ year established destination offers both day tours and overnight accommodations with lush garden landscapes and private cottages.
